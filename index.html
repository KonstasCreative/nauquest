Explore Aegina - Hydra and Poros

Step onto Aegina, a treasure trove of ancient wonders and vibrant local life. Visit the “Acropolis” of Aegina, indulge in the island’s delicious pistachio sweets, and wander through its picturesque seaside villages. 

Sail into Poros, the island of lush pine forests and tranquil waters. Known for its rich maritime heritage and vibrant waterfront, Poros is the perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural discovery, waiting to be explored.

Lose yourself in the charm of Hydra, an island where time stands still! With its car-free cobblestone streets, elegant mansions, and stories etched into every corner, Hydra offers a serene escape into Greece’s timeless beauty and history!
